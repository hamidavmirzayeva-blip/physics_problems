**Step 1: Formula for momentum**  
Momentum: `p = m * v`  
where:  
- `p` = momentum (kg·m/s)  
- `m` = mass (kg)  
- `v` = velocity (m/s)  

**Step 2: Convert masses to kilograms**  
- Fly: 2 grams → 0.002 kg  
- Tennis ball: 60 grams → 0.060 kg  

**Step 3: Calculate momentum**  
- Fly: `p_fly = 0.002 * 10 = 0.02 kg·m/s`  
- Tennis ball: `p_ball = 0.060 * 1 = 0.06 kg·m/s`  

**Step 4: Compare**  
- `p_ball = 0.06 > 0.02 = p_fly`  
✅ The tennis ball has greater momentum.
