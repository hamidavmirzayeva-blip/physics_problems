

We are given the **positions as functions of time**.

Alice’s position:
A(t) = (2 + t , 8 − 3t)

Bob’s position:
B(t) = (2t − 1 , 2t + 2)

Each pair represents **(x, y) coordinates** at time **t**.

---

# 1. When do they collide?

For a collision, they must be **at the same place at the same time**.

So both coordinates must be equal.

### x–coordinates

2 + t = 2t − 1

Solve:

2 + t = 2t − 1

2 + 1 = 2t − t

3 = t

t = **3**

---

### y–coordinates

8 − 3t = 2t + 2

Solve:

8 − 3t = 2t + 2

8 − 2 = 2t + 3t

6 = 5t

t = **6/5**

---

# 2. Compare the times

From x–equation:

t = 3

From y–equation:

t = 6/5 = 1.2

They are **not equal**, so the two coordinates cannot match at the same time.

✅ Therefore **Alice and Bob do NOT collide**.

---

# 3. Find the distance between them

Distance between two points:

distance = √[(x₁ − x₂)² + (y₁ − y₂)²]

Alice:

x₁ = 2 + t
y₁ = 8 − 3t

Bob:

x₂ = 2t − 1
y₂ = 2t + 2

---

### Difference in x

x₁ − x₂ = (2 + t) − (2t − 1)

= 2 + t − 2t + 1

= **3 − t**

---

### Difference in y

y₁ − y₂ = (8 − 3t) − (2t + 2)

= 8 − 3t − 2t − 2

= **6 − 5t**

---

# 4. Distance formula

d(t) = √[(3 − t)² + (6 − 5t)²]

To find the **minimum distance**, minimize the square:

D(t) = (3 − t)² + (6 − 5t)²

---

# 5. Expand

(3 − t)² = 9 − 6t + t²

(6 − 5t)² = 36 − 60t + 25t²

Add them:

D(t) = 9 − 6t + t² + 36 − 60t + 25t²

D(t) = **26t² − 66t + 45**

---

# 6. Find minimum

Take derivative:

D'(t) = 52t − 66

Set equal to zero:

52t − 66 = 0

52t = 66

t = **66/52**

t ≈ **1.27**

---

# 7. Minimum distance

Substitute t ≈ 1.27

3 − t ≈ 1.73

6 − 5t ≈ −0.35

Distance:

d = √[(1.73)² + (−0.35)²]

d ≈ √(2.99 + 0.12)

d ≈ √3.11

d ≈ **1.76**

---

# Final Answer

• The paths **do not intersect**, so Alice and Bob **do not collide**.

• The **minimum distance** between them is about **1.76 units**.

• It occurs at time **t ≈ 1.27**.
