# Gravity at ISS Altitude and Why Astronauts Feel Weightless

---

## Given Information

Earth’s surface gravity:

$$
g_0 = 9.81\ \text{m/s}^2
$$

Earth radius:

$$
R = 6378\ \text{km}
$$

ISS altitude:

$$
h = 400\ \text{km}
$$

Distance from Earth’s center:

$$
r = R + h = 6778\ \text{km}
$$

---

## 2. Formula for Gravity at Altitude

From Newton’s law of gravitation:

$$
g = \frac{GM}{r^2}
$$

Relative to Earth’s surface:

$$
g = g_0 \left(\frac{R}{R+h}\right)^2
$$

---

## 3. Calculation

Substitute values:

$$
g = 9.81 \left(\frac{6378}{6778}\right)^2
$$

Compute ratio:

$$
\frac{6378}{6778} \approx 0.941
$$

Square it:

$$
(0.941)^2 \approx 0.885
$$

Multiply:

$$
g = 9.81 \times 0.885
$$

$$
g \approx 8.68\ \text{m/s}^2
$$

---

## 4. Final Answer

$$
\boxed{g_{\text{ISS}} \approx 8.7\ \text{m/s}^2}
$$

This is about:

$$
\approx 0.88\, g_0
$$

---

## 5. Why Astronauts Feel Weightless

Astronauts are not outside gravity. At ISS altitude, gravity is still strong:

$$
g \approx 8.7\ \text{m/s}^2
$$

However, they experience **apparent weightlessness**.

---

## 6. Free Fall Explanation

The ISS is:

- Moving sideways very fast
- Being pulled downward by gravity

So its motion is continuous free fall:

$$
\text{ISS is constantly falling toward Earth}
$$

But:

$$
\text{It moves forward fast enough to keep missing Earth}
$$

---

## 7. Why Weight Disappears

Weight is the normal force:

- On Earth:
  
$$
W = mg \quad \text{(ground pushes up on you)}
$$

- In ISS:
  
$$
W_{\text{apparent}} = 0
$$

because:

- Astronauts and the ISS fall together
- No supporting surface force acts on them

---

## 8. Key Idea

Even though:

$$
g \neq 0
$$

they feel weightless because:

$$
\text{Weightlessness = free fall condition, not zero gravity}
$$

---

## 9. Final Summary

- Gravity at ISS altitude:
  
$$
g \approx 8.7\ \text{m/s}^2
$$

- Reason for weightlessness:
  
$$
\text{Continuous orbital free fall with no normal force}
$$

$$
\boxed{\text{Astronauts feel weightless even though gravity is strong}}
$$
