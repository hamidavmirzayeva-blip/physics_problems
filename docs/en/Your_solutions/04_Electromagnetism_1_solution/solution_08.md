# 🧲 Lorentz Force on a Moving Charge

## 📌 Given
- Charge: q = 2 × 10⁻¹⁹ C  
- Mass: m = 4 × 10⁻²⁷ kg (not needed for force)  
- Magnetic field: B = 0.5 T  
- Speed: v = 10⁶ m/s  
- Motion is **perpendicular** to magnetic field (θ = 90°)

---

# ⚡ Step 1: Lorentz Force Formula

Magnetic part of Lorentz force:

F = qvB sinθ

Since θ = 90°:
sinθ = 1

So:

F = qvB

---

# 🧮 Step 2: Substitute values

F = (2 × 10⁻¹⁹) × (10⁶) × (0.5)

---

# 🔢 Step 3: Calculate

First multiply numbers:

2 × 0.5 = 1

So:

F = 1 × 10⁻¹⁹ × 10⁶

Combine powers of 10:

10⁻¹⁹ × 10⁶ = 10⁻¹³

---

# ✅ Final Answer

F = 1 × 10⁻¹³ N
