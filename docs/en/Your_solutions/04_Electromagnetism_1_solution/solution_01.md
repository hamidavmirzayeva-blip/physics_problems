# 🧲 Electric Force on a Charge at the Center of a Square

## 📌 Problem
Four point charges of +1.0 C are placed at the corners of a square of side 1.0 m.  
Find the magnitude and direction of the electric force on a charge of -2.0 C at the center.

---

## 📐 Distance from Center to Corner

Diagonal of square:
d = √(1² + 1²) = √2

Distance to center:
r = √2 / 2

---

## ⚡ Force from One Charge

Coulomb’s Law:
F = k |q₁q₂| / r²

Substitute:
F = 9 × 10⁹ × (1 × 2) / ( (√2 / 2)² )

(√2 / 2)² = 1/2

F = 9 × 10⁹ × 2 / (1/2)

F = 3.6 × 10¹⁰ N

---

## 📊 Components

Fx = F / √2  
Fy = F / √2  

---

## 🔄 Net Force

ΣFx = 0  
ΣFy = 0  

---

## ✅ Final Answer

F_net = 0 N  

Direction: none
