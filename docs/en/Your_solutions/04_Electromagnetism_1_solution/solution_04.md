# ⚛️ Electric vs Gravitational Force in a Hydrogen Atom

## 📌 Problem
Find:
- Electric force (Fe)
- Gravitational force (Fg)
- Ratio Fe / Fg  

Given:
r = 5.3 × 10⁻¹¹ m  

Constants:
k = 9 × 10⁹ N·m²/C²  
G = 6.67 × 10⁻¹¹ N·m²/kg²  
e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C  
mₑ = 9.11 × 10⁻³¹ kg  
mₚ = 1.67 × 10⁻²⁷ kg  

---

## ⚡ Electric Force

Formula:
Fe = k * e² / r²  

Substitute:
Fe = (9 × 10⁹) × (1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹)² / (5.3 × 10⁻¹¹)²  

Step-by-step:
(1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹)² = 2.56 × 10⁻³⁸  
(5.3 × 10⁻¹¹)² ≈ 2.81 × 10⁻²¹  

Fe ≈ (9 × 10⁹ × 2.56 × 10⁻³⁸) / (2.81 × 10⁻²¹)  

Fe ≈ 8.2 × 10⁻⁸ N  

---

## 🌍 Gravitational Force

Formula:
Fg = G * mₑ * mₚ / r²  

Substitute:
Fg = (6.67 × 10⁻¹¹) × (9.11 × 10⁻³¹ × 1.67 × 10⁻²⁷) / (2.81 × 10⁻²¹)  

Step-by-step:
mₑ × mₚ ≈ 1.52 × 10⁻⁵⁷  

Fg ≈ (6.67 × 10⁻¹¹ × 1.52 × 10⁻⁵⁷) / (2.81 × 10⁻²¹)  

Fg ≈ 3.6 × 10⁻⁴⁷ N  

---

## 🔢 Ratio

Fe / Fg = (8.2 × 10⁻⁸) / (3.6 × 10⁻⁴⁷)  

Fe / Fg ≈ 2.3 × 10³⁹  

---

## ✅ Final Answer

Electric force:
Fe ≈ 8.2 × 10⁻⁸ N  

Gravitational force:
Fg ≈ 3.6 × 10⁻⁴⁷ N  

Ratio:
Fe / Fg ≈ 2.3 × 10³⁹  

---

## 🧠 Conclusion

The electric force is about **10³⁹ times stronger** than gravity at the atomic scale.  
This is why gravity is negligible inside atoms.
