# 🧲 Magnetic Force on a Moving Proton

## 📌 Given
- Velocity: v = (2 î − 4 ĵ + k̂) m/s  
- Magnetic field: B = (î + 2 ĵ − k̂) T  
- Charge of proton: q = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C  

---

# ⚡ Step 1: Lorentz Force Formula

Magnetic force:

F = q |v × B|

So we first compute the cross product v × B.

---

# 📐 Step 2: Compute v × B

| î   ĵ   k̂ |
|-----|-----|-----|
| 2   -4    1 |
| 1    2   -1 |

---

## Expand determinant:

### î component:
(-4)(-1) − (1)(2) = 4 − 2 = 2

### ĵ component:
- [ (2)(-1) − (1)(1) ]  
= - [ -2 − 1 ]  
= 3

### k̂ component:
(2)(2) − (-4)(1) = 4 + 4 = 8

---

## So:

v × B = (2 î + 3 ĵ + 8 k̂)

---

# 📊 Step 3: Magnitude of cross product

|v × B| = √(2² + 3² + 8²)

= √(4 + 9 + 64)

= √77

≈ 8.77

---

# ⚡ Step 4: Magnetic force

F = q |v × B|

F = (1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹)(8.77)

F ≈ 1.40 × 10⁻¹⁸ N

---

# ✅ Final Answer

F ≈ 1.4 × 10⁻¹⁸ N
