# 🧲 Electric Potential at the Center of a Square

## 📌 Problem
Point charges of +1 C, -2 C, +3 C, and -4 C are placed at the corners of a square of side 1.0 m.  
Find the electric potential at the center.

---

## 📐 Distance from Center to Corner

Diagonal of square:
d = √(1² + 1²) = √2

Distance to center:
r = √2 / 2

---

## ⚡ Electric Potential Formula

V = k * Σ(q / r)

Since all charges are at the same distance:
V = k / r * (q₁ + q₂ + q₃ + q₄)

---

## 🔢 Sum of Charges

q_total = +1 - 2 + 3 - 4  
q_total = -2 C

---

## 🧮 Calculation

V = 9 × 10⁹ * (-2) / (√2 / 2)

Divide by (√2 / 2):
= multiply by (2 / √2)

V = 9 × 10⁹ * (-2) * (2 / √2)

V = -36 × 10⁹ / √2

V ≈ -2.55 × 10¹⁰ V

---

## ✅ Final Answer

Electric potential at the center:

V ≈ -2.55 × 10¹⁰ V
