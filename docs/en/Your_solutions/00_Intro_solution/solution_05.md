A vector **A** has:

* Magnitude **15**
* Angle **60°** with the horizontal axis

To find components:

Horizontal component:
**Aₓ = A · cos(θ)**

Vertical component:
**Aᵧ = A · sin(θ)**

---

### 1. Horizontal component

Aₓ = 15 · cos(60°)

cos(60°) = 0.5

Aₓ = 15 × 0.5 = **7.5**

---

### 2. Vertical component

Aᵧ = 15 · sin(60°)

sin(60°) ≈ 0.866

Aᵧ = 15 × 0.866 ≈ **12.99 ≈ 13**

---

✅ **Final Answer**

Horizontal component: **Aₓ = 7.5**
Vertical component: **Aᵧ ≈ 13**
