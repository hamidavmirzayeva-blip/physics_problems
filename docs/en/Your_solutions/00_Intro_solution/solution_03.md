**F' = G · ((m₁/2)(m₂/2)) / (2r)²**

(m₁/2)(m₂/2) = **m₁m₂ / 4**

---

### 3. Simplify the distance

(2r)² = **4r²**

---

### 4. Substitute back

**F' = G · (m₁m₂ / 4) / (4r²)**

Multiply the denominators:

**4 × 4 = 16**

So:

**F' = G · m₁m₂ / (16r²)**

Original:
