Given formula:

**T = 2π √(L / g)**

We need to rearrange it to make **g** the subject.

---

### Step 1: Remove the square root

Square both sides.

**T² = (2π)² × (L / g)**

---

### Step 2: Simplify

(2π)² = **4π²**

So:

**T² = 4π²L / g**

---

### Step 3: Solve for g

Multiply both sides by **g**:

**gT² = 4π²L**

Now divide by **T²**:

**g = 4π²L / T²**

---

✅ **Final Formula:**

**g = (4π²L) / T²**
