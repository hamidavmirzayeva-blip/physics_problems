# Echo and Distance to a Cliff

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo after a certain time. We want to find the distance to the cliff.

---

## Formula

The basic relation between distance, speed, and time is:

$$
\text{distance} = \text{speed} \times \text{time}
$$

> **Important:** The time measured is the **round-trip time** for the sound (to the cliff and back).  
> So if $d$ is the distance to the cliff:

$$
2d = v \cdot t
$$

Where:  
- $d$ = distance to the cliff  
- $v$ = speed of sound in air  
- $t$ = total time for the echo  

---

## Given

- $v = 343\ \text{m/s}$  
- $t = 1\ \text{s}$  

---

## Calculation

$$
d = \frac{v \cdot t}{2} = \frac{343 \cdot 1}{2} = 171.5\ \text{m}
$$

---

## ✅ Answer

The cliff is **171.5 meters away**.
