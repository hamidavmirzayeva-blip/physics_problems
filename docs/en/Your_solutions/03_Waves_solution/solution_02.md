# Guitar String Wave Speed

**Given:**  
- Length of string: $L = 64 \ \text{cm} = 0.64 \ \text{m}$  
- Fundamental frequency: $f_1 = 330 \ \text{Hz}$  

---

## Step 1: Wavelength for the fundamental

For a string fixed at both ends, the fundamental mode has **one antinode** (and two nodes at the ends).  
The wavelength is:

$$
\lambda_1 = 2L
$$

So:

$$
\lambda_1 = 2 \cdot 0.64 = 1.28 \ \text{m}
$$

---

## Step 2: Wave speed formula

The speed of a wave on a string is:

$$
v = f \cdot \lambda
$$

---

## Step 3: Calculate wave speed

$$
v = f_1 \cdot \lambda_1 = 330 \cdot 1.28 \approx 422.4 \ \text{m/s}
$$

---

## ✅ Answer

The speed of the wave on the string is approximately:

$$
\boxed{v \approx 422 \ \text{m/s}}
$$
