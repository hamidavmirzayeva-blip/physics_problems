# Propagation of Error I

Given:

$$
r = (6.20 \pm 0.05)\ \text{cm}
$$

## Step 1: Volume of a sphere

Formula:

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3
$$

Substitute \( r = 6.20 \):

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi (6.20)^3
$$

$$
(6.20)^3 = 238.328
$$

$$
V \approx \frac{4}{3}\pi \cdot 238.328 \approx 997.7\ \text{cm}^3
$$

$$
V \approx 998\ \text{cm}^3
$$

---

## Step 2: Uncertainty in volume

Propagation formula:

$$
\Delta V = \left|\frac{dV}{dr}\right|\Delta r
$$

Differentiate:

$$
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 \Rightarrow \frac{dV}{dr} = 4\pi r^2
$$

Substitute values:

$$
\Delta V = 4\pi (6.20)^2 (0.05)
$$

$$
(6.20)^2 = 38.44
$$

$$
\Delta V = 4\pi \cdot 38.44 \cdot 0.05 = 7.688\pi
$$

$$
\Delta V \approx 24.2\ \text{cm}^3
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
V = (998 \pm 24)\ \text{cm}^3
$$
