# Percentage Uncertainty

## Given

A time measurement is:

$$
t = 5.45 \pm 0.22 \ \text{s}
$$

---

## Step 1: Formula for percentage uncertainty

$$
\text{Percentage uncertainty} = \frac{\text{absolute uncertainty}}{\text{measured value}} \times 100\%
$$

---

## Step 2: Substitute values

$$
\text{Percentage uncertainty} = \frac{0.22}{5.45} \times 100\%
$$

---

## Step 3: Calculate

$$
\text{Percentage uncertainty} \approx 4.04\%
$$

---
