# Speed of Light in Diamond

Given:

- Refractive index of diamond:

$$
n = 2.42
$$

- Speed of light in vacuum:

$$
c = 3.0 \times 10^8\ \text{m/s}
$$

---

# Formula

The speed of light in a medium is given by:

$$
n = \frac{c}{v}
$$

Rearranging:

$$
v = \frac{c}{n}
$$

---

# Substitution

$$
v = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8}{2.42}
$$

---

# Calculation

$$
v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8\ \text{m/s}
$$

---

# Final Answer

$$
\boxed{
v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8\ \text{m/s}
}
$$

---

# Conclusion

The speed of light in diamond is significantly lower than in vacuum due to its high refractive index.
