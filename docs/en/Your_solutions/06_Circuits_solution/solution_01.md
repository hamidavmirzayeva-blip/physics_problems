# Resistors in Series and Parallel

Given:

- $R_1 = 15\ \Omega$
- $R_2 = 30\ \Omega$
- $R_3 = 50\ \Omega$
- Battery voltage: $V = 12\text{ V}$

---

# 1. Series Connection

## Equivalent Resistance

For resistors connected in series:

$$
R_{eq} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
R_{eq} = 15 + 30 + 50
$$

$$
R_{eq} = 95\ \Omega
$$

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{R_{eq} = 95\ \Omega}
$$

---

## Current from the Battery

Using Ohm’s Law:

$$
I = \frac{V}{R}
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
I = \frac{12}{95}
$$

$$
I \approx 0.126\text{ A}
$$

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{I \approx 0.13\text{ A}}
$$

---

# 2. Parallel Connection

## Equivalent Resistance

For resistors connected in parallel:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{eq}} =
\frac{1}{R_1} +
\frac{1}{R_2} +
\frac{1}{R_3}
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{eq}} =
\frac{1}{15} +
\frac{1}{30} +
\frac{1}{50}
$$

Find a common denominator:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{eq}} =
\frac{10 + 5 + 3}{150}
$$

$$
\frac{1}{R_{eq}} =
\frac{18}{150}
$$

Simplify:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{eq}} =
\frac{3}{25}
$$

Invert both sides:

$$
R_{eq} = \frac{25}{3}
$$

$$
R_{eq} \approx 8.33\ \Omega
$$

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{R_{eq} \approx 8.33\ \Omega}
$$

---

## Current from the Battery

Using Ohm’s Law:

$$
I = \frac{V}{R}
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
I = \frac{12}{8.33}
$$

$$
I \approx 1.44\text{ A}
$$

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{I \approx 1.44\text{ A}}
$$
