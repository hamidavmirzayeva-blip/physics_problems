# Power and Energy in a Resistor

Given:

- Resistance:

$$
R = 100\ \Omega
$$

- Voltage applied:

$$
V = 50\text{ V}
$$

- Time:

$$
t = 5\text{ minutes}
$$

Convert time to seconds:

$$
t = 5 \times 60
$$

$$
t = 300\text{ s}
$$

---

# 1. Power Dissipated

The power dissipated by a resistor is given by:

$$
P = \frac{V^2}{R}
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
P = \frac{50^2}{100}
$$

$$
P = \frac{2500}{100}
$$

$$
P = 25\text{ W}
$$

---

# Final Answer for Power

$$
\boxed{
P = 25\text{ W}
}
$$

---

# 2. Energy Consumed

Energy is calculated using:

$$
E = Pt
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
E = 25 \times 300
$$

$$
E = 7500\text{ J}
$$

---

# Final Answer for Energy

$$
\boxed{
E = 7500\text{ J}
}
$$

---

# Conclusion

- The resistor dissipates:

$$
25\text{ W}
$$

- The total energy consumed in 5 minutes is:

$$
7500\text{ J}
$$
